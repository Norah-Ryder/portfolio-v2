Source Logs

The evidence layer: the redacted transcripts the walkthroughs quote from.

These are redacted source exhibits, not standalone claims. Model turns are reproduced verbatim; personal content is masked; and the highlighted passages are keyed to the quotes in the walkthroughs — they are the evidence the walkthroughs cite. A “src” link on a quoted passage jumps straight to its highlighted span here; this page is the browsable way in.

Every quoted line is checked verbatim against the raw export by an automated gate, and every quoted piece of evidence must resolve to its highlighted span or the build fails. The small set of author-voice phrases exempt from these checks is published and reviewable.
